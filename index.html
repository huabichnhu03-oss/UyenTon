PROJECT METHODOLOGY

The Architecture Experience Structure

A breakdown of professional methodology for managing projects from conception through to municipal approval, ensuring all aesthetic and regulatory constraints are met.

01

Site Assessment & Context

Conducting rigorous site measurements and inspections prior to and post-construction. Developing site-responsive layouts that heavily consider existing topography, solar orientation, and local wind patterns to ensure sustainable design integration.

02

Schematic & Design Development

Drafting and modifying detailed schematic drawing packages. Utilizing AutoCAD and Revit to edit project settings, drafting objects, components, and sheet layouts to maintain a highly efficient project workflow.

03

OBC & Zoning Compliance

Interpreting and strictly applying the Ontario Building Code (OBC) and local zoning by-laws. Producing detailed compliance reports that successfully streamline municipal planning approvals, Minor Variances, and Site Plan Approvals.

04

Technical Detailing & Millwork

Designing precise 2D drawings and 3D models for interior elements, specifically cabinetry and complex millwork for residential and commercial spaces. Assisting with material selection and technical specifications.

05

3D Visualization & Delivery

Creating highly detailed 3D renderings and visualizations using SketchUp, Lumion, and V-ray. These models are crucial for enhancing client engagement, verifying construction accuracy, and ensuring the built environment perfectly matches the design intent.
